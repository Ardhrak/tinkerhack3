Game Rules

  • Select your preferred difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Read the movie quote carefully.
  • You have 1 attempt to guess each movie correctly.
  • Score 10 points for each correct answer.
  • Try to complete all quotes in your chosen difficulty level.
  • The game ends when you've gone through all quotes or want to try a different level.
  • All the letters of movie guesses must be in capital
  • Correct spelling must be given
